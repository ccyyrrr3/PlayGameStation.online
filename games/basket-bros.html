About Basket Bros

Basket Bros is a fun-filled multiplayer basketball game that combines skill, strategy, and hilarious physics. Challenge your friends or play against random opponents online as you aim to outscore them with spectacular dunks and impressive moves.

The game features simple controls but deep gameplay, allowing for both casual fun and competitive matches. With its unique physics system, every match is unpredictable and full of laugh-out-loud moments!

Game Features

  • Multiplayer Gameplay: Play with friends locally or challenge players online
  • Wacky Physics: Unpredictable and hilarious physics-based gameplay
  • Simple Controls: Easy to learn but difficult to master
  • Various Game Modes: 1v1, 2v2, and tournament options

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • WASD / Arrows Move your character around the court
  • W / Up / Space Jump to shoot or block
  • Mouse Hold and release to shoot the ball

Game Rules

  • Grab Ball: Touch the ball to pick it up automatically
  • Shoot: Press and hold the mouse button, then release to shoot
  • Block: Jump in front of opponents to block their shots
  • Steal: Touch an opponent holding the ball to steal it
  • Scoring: The player or team with the most points when time expires wins!

Pro Tip: Master the timing of your jumps and shots to become unstoppable on the court. Practice makes perfect!
