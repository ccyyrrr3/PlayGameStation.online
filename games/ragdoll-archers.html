Ragdoll Archers

Archery Multiplayer Physics

Engage in hilarious archery battles with ragdoll physics. Take aim, fire, and watch the chaos unfold in this fun multiplayer game.

About the Game

Ragdoll Archers is a hilarious multiplayer archery game featuring ragdoll physics. Players take turns shooting arrows at each other, with the goal of knocking their opponent off the platform or dealing enough damage to win.

The game's physics-based mechanics create unpredictable and entertaining moments, making each match unique and full of surprises. With various weapons and power-ups available, the gameplay remains fresh and exciting.

Game Features

  • Ragdoll physics for hilarious gameplay
  • Multiplayer battles with friends
  • Various weapons and power-ups
  • Different game modes
  • Customizable characters
  • Fun and chaotic battles

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Aim Move your mouse to aim your bow
  • Power Hold left mouse button to charge your shot
  • Shoot Release to fire your arrow
  • Move Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character

Game Rules

The objective is to defeat your opponent:

  • Take turns shooting arrows at each other
  • Knock your opponent off the platform to win
  • Deal enough damage to win the round
  • Use power-ups strategically
  • Win multiple rounds to win the match
