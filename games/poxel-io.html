About Poxel.io

Poxel.io is a multiplayer voxel-based game where players compete against each other in a colorful, blocky world. Build structures, collect resources, and battle other players in this fast-paced action game that combines elements of building and combat.

With its unique combination of construction and competitive gameplay, Poxel.io offers an engaging experience that rewards both creativity and combat skills. Adapt your strategy on the fly, build defensive structures, or go on the offensive against other players.

Game Features

  • Real-time multiplayer battles with players worldwide
  • Creative building mechanics for defense and strategy
  • Various weapons and tools to defeat opponents
  • Colorful voxel-based graphics with unique style
  • Different game modes to keep gameplay fresh
  • Resource collection and management systems

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • WASD Move your character around the world
  • Left Click Place blocks to build structures
  • Right Click Attack other players and structures
  • Space Jump over obstacles
  • 1-9 Switch between different blocks and tools

Game Rules

Your objective is to dominate the arena:

  • Collect resources from the environment to build with
  • Build structures for defense or tactical advantage
  • Eliminate other players to gain points and resources
  • Survive as long as possible to climb the leaderboard
  • Adapt your strategy based on the current game mode
