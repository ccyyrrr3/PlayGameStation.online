About the Game

8 Ball Billiards Classic is a realistic pool simulation game that brings the excitement of billiards right to your browser. With stunning graphics, realistic physics, and smooth gameplay, this game offers an authentic billiards experience for players of all skill levels.

Game Features:

  • Realistic physics engine for authentic ball movement
  • Beautiful 3D graphics with detailed table and environment
  • Multiple game modes including 8-ball and practice mode
  • Intuitive controls for precise shot placement
  • Adjustable difficulty levels for players of all skill levels

How to Play

Basic Controls:

  • Aim Move your mouse to aim the cue
  • Power Hold left mouse button and drag back to set power
  • Shoot Release the left mouse button
  • Place Click to position after a scratch
  • View Right-click and drag to look around

Game Rules:

  • Players take turns shooting until they miss or commit a foul
  • Sink all your assigned balls (stripes or solids) before the 8-ball
  • Sinking the 8-ball before clearing your balls results in a loss
  • Sinking the cue ball while shooting at the 8-ball is a loss
  • You must call the pocket when shooting the 8-ball
