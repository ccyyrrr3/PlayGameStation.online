Draw Climber

Draw legs for your character and race to the finish line in this creative physics puzzle game!

About Draw Climber

Draw Climber is a unique physics-based puzzle game where you draw legs for your character to help them navigate through challenging obstacle courses. Your drawing skills and creativity will determine how well your character can climb, jump, and race to the finish line.

With simple mechanics but deep gameplay possibilities, Draw Climber challenges you to think creatively about how shapes affect movement and balance. Each level presents new obstacles that require different leg designs to overcome.

How to Play

  • Drawing: Use your mouse or touchscreen to draw legs for your character
  • Design: The shape of your drawing affects how your character moves
  • Mechanics: Your legs will rotate, propelling your character forward
  • Goal: Reach the finish line as quickly as possible
  • Challenge: Different obstacles require different leg designs

Experiment with different shapes and sizes to find the most effective design for each level's unique challenges!

Why Players Love Draw Climber

Problem-Solving
