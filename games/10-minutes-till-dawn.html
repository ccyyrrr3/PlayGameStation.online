10 Minutes Till Dawn

Survive for 10 minutes in this intense action game against waves of enemies. Upgrade your weapons and abilities to last longer.

About the Game

10 Minutes Till Dawn is an intense survival game where your goal is to survive for 10 minutes against increasingly difficult waves of enemies. The game features a unique upgrade system that allows you to enhance your weapons and abilities as you progress.

With each passing minute, the enemies become stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategy. Choose your upgrades wisely to create powerful combinations that will help you survive until dawn.

Game Features

  • Intense 10-minute survival gameplay
  • Multiple character classes to choose from
  • Diverse weapon and ability upgrades
  • Increasing difficulty with each minute
  • Strategic gameplay elements
  • Addictive progression system

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Move Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Aim Move your mouse to aim your weapon
  • Shoot Left-click to fire your weapon
  • Upgrade Choose upgrades when leveling up

Game Rules

The objective is to survive for 10 minutes:

  • Survive against waves of enemies
  • Collect experience points to level up
  • Choose upgrades to enhance your abilities
  • Defeat enemies to gain resources
  • Last until dawn to win
