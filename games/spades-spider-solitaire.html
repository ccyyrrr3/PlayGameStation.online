About the Game

Spades Spider Solitaire combines the traditional Spider Solitaire gameplay with a Spades theme. This addictive card game challenges you to arrange all cards in descending order by suit. Clear the board by building complete sequences from King to Ace and improve your strategic thinking skills.

Whether you're a solitaire expert or new to card games, Spades Spider Solitaire offers an engaging experience that will keep you entertained for hours. The game features multiple difficulty levels to match your skill level.

Game Features

  • Classic Spider Solitaire gameplay
  • Beautiful card designs with Spades theme
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Undo feature for fixing mistakes
  • Hint system for when you're stuck
  • Game timer to track your progress

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Click and drag cards to move them
  • Arrange cards in descending order
  • Click the deck to deal more cards when available
  • Use the undo button to revert your last move

Game Rules

Spider Solitaire follows these basic rules:

  • Cards can be placed on cards of one rank higher (e.g., 5 on 6)
  • Complete sequences from King to Ace are automatically removed
  • Empty columns can be filled with any card or sequence
  • You can only move visible cards at the bottom of each column
  • Win by removing all cards from the table
