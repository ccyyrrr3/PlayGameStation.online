Traffic Rider VVQ

Racing Motorcycle 3D Single Player

Race through traffic on your motorcycle in this adrenaline-pumping racing game with realistic physics.

About the Game

Game Overview

Traffic Rider VVQ is an exciting motorcycle racing game that challenges players to weave through traffic at high speeds. Experience the thrill of riding through increasingly difficult roads and weather conditions while avoiding crashes and completing objectives.

With its realistic graphics, responsive controls, and engaging progression system, Traffic Rider VVQ offers an immersive racing experience. Unlock new motorcycles, customize your ride, and challenge yourself with different game modes as you become a master rider.

Game Features

  • Multiple motorcycles to unlock
  • Various racing environments and weather
  • Different game modes and challenges
  • Realistic physics and controls
  • Progression and upgrade system
  • Impressive graphics and sound effects

How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Accelerate W key or Up Arrow
  • Brake S key or Down Arrow
  • Steer A/D keys or Left/Right Arrows
  • Horn H key

Game Rules

Your objective is to race successfully:

  • Navigate through traffic without crashing
  • Complete distance objectives
  • Earn points for close calls and high speeds
  • Unlock new motorcycles and tracks
  • Try different game modes for varied challenges
